Help Page

Welcome to the Opinion Mining AI app! This app is designed to analyze the sentiment of text data using natural language processing techniques. Here's a brief overview of how to use the app:

  • Login or Register: To use the app, you need to either login or register for an account. If you don't have an account, you can register by clicking the "Register" button and providing a username and password. If you already have an account, you can login by providing your username and password.
  • Dashboard: Once you are logged in, you will be redirected to the dashboard page. Here, you can upload a text file or enter text manually to analyze its sentiment. The app will display a graph showing the overall sentiment of the text, as well as a breakdown of the sentiment for each sentence in the text.
  • Sentiment Analysis: The app uses the TextBlob library to perform sentiment analysis on the text. The sentiment score ranges from -1 (negative) to 1 (positive), with 0 being neutral. The app also displays the polarity and subjectivity of the text.
  • Logout: To logout, click the "Logout" button in the top right corner of the app. This will clear your session state and redirect you to the login/register page.

If you have any questions or feedback, please contact us at opinionminingai@example.com.
